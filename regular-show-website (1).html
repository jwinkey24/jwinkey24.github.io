About Regular Show

Regular Show follows the hilarious adventures of Mordecai (a blue jay) and Rigby (a raccoon), two best friends working as groundskeepers at a local park. Their seemingly mundane job is constantly interrupted by wild, supernatural, and totally epic adventures!

Created by J.G. Quintel, this Cartoon Network show ran from 2010 to 2017 and became a cult classic for its quirky humor and relatable characters.

Main Characters:

  • Mordecai - The cool blue jay
  • Rigby - The hyperactive raccoon
  • Benson - The angry gumball machine manager
  • Skips - The immortal yeti groundskeeper
  • Pops - The lollipop-headed park owner
